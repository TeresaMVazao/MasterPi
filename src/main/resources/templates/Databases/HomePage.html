Databases

This is the databases page where all the information that has been gathered regarding the several games, sensors, visitors and participants can be seen. From here, operations on the several databases can be performed so as to see and/or manipulate data.

Games Databases

This is where all the databases corresponding to each game that is currently deployed or has been deployed in the museum can be checked to see the leaderboards of that game, the players that have participated in these games as well as many other pieces of information regarding them.

Sensors Databases

This is where sensors databases can viewed. These databases contain past information regarding the values acquired by the several types of sensors (Temperature, Humidity, Light, Sound, etc...). These can then be used to study the environmental conditions of the museum and make adjustments accordingly.

Sensors Databases »

Devices Database

This is where the several types of devices that are deployed in the museum can be consulted in order to see their source code (if applicable), their diagrams (if applicable), their CAD Files (if applicable) and many other relevant pieces of information regarding the devices.
