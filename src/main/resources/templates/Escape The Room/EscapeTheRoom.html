Escape The Room

The Escape The Room game is the very first interaction that was deployed in the Peddy Room System and consists of several microcontrollers, each one executing a certain mini-games that awards players with points and clues so they can progress throughout the Escape The Room and complete it within the given time of 1 hour

New Game

Create a new Escape The Room games with at most 4 participants and at least 2 of them. The games will have a duration of 1 hour and can be terminated at any time. The Sooner players finish, the bigger their score.
